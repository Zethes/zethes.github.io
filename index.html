Welcome

Welcome to the page of the Zethes Organization. Zethes is a small open source group hoping to make something you will find useful. There is a strong focus on gaming and cross-platform-ness in our projects. We specialize in C and C++, but sometimes dabble in similar languages such as C# or D.
